Welcome Guardian.

Destiny Item Manager is here to help you become a more powerful Guardian.

The first step is for you to allow DIM to know more about your characters, vault, and items in Destiny. We also need permission to move your items in Destiny.

A Bungie.net account that is linked to either a PlayStation or Microsoft account is required. Learn more about Bungie.net accounts, and linking your PlayStation and Xbox Accounts.

Having trouble authorizing DIM with Bungie.net? Reach out to us on Twitter for help.
